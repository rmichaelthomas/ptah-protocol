# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

**Summary:** Be direct, be constructive, be kind. Engage in good faith. Criticism of ideas is welcome; personal attacks are not.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **protocol@ptah.world**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
